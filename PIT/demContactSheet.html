These are pictures of Democrats (two pictures of each person). Please take a minute to read the descriptions below. This will help you remember each person, and their political alignment.

Joe Biden was the 47th Vice President of the United States, during President Barack Obama's terms. He is pro-life, and was known for his diplomatic ability both inside the U.S. legislature and on the world-stage.

Al Gore was the 45th Vice President of the United States, during President Bill Clinton's terms. He is known for his work on environmental issues.

Bill Clinton was the 42nd President of the United States. In his first term, he advanced pro-choice legislation and resisted tax cuts.

Jimmy Carter was the 39th President of the United States. He created the Department of Energy and the Department of Education.

To help you get comfortable with the procedure you will first practice sorting words into two groups, GOOD words and BAD words, as shown below. Make sure you are familiar with each group before continuing. *n *n GOOD: PLEASANT, DELIGHT, HELPFUL, JOY, BEAUTIFUL, WONDERFUL, ENJOY, CHEERFUL, SUCCESS. *n *n BAD: HORRIBLE, ANGRY, TERRIBLE, TRAGIC, HATE, DESTROY, BRUTAL, DISASTER, UGLY.
